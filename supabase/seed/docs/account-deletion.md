# Deleting Your Account (GDPR)

Use this procedure when a creator asks to permanently delete their account and data, including GDPR erasure requests.

## What deletion does
- Deletion removes the workspace, posts, Library, storefront, and personal data. It is permanent and cannot be undone.
- A short grace window applies before data is purged from backups.

## Steps
1. Confirm the requester is the workspace **Owner** and verify identity against account details.
2. Advise them to export first if they want a copy (see *Exporting Your Data*).
3. In **Settings → Account → Delete account**, have them type the confirmation phrase and submit. The account deactivates immediately.

## Timeline and cleanup
- Active data is removed at once; the storefront and custom domain stop serving.
- Backups holding personal data are purged within 30 days to satisfy erasure requirements.

## Escalate when
- The request comes from someone other than the Owner, or names a specific legal/GDPR deadline.
