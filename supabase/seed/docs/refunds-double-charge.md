# Refunds and Double Charges

Use this procedure when a customer sees the same purchase charged twice, or is waiting on a refund the merchant says was already issued.

## First, tell them apart
- A **pending** duplicate is often an authorization hold that drops off in 3-5 business days — no action needed.
- Two **posted** charges for the same amount and merchant is a true double charge worth investigating.

## Steps
1. Open the account and compare the two entries — check status (pending vs. posted), amount, and timestamp.
2. If one is a pending hold, explain it will fall off automatically and confirm the date.
3. For two posted charges, contact the merchant first; if unresolved, open a billing dispute for the duplicate.

## Refund not showing
- Merchant refunds post 5-10 business days after the merchant releases them; ask for the refund date or ARN.
- Confirm the refund is going to the same card that was charged.

## Escalate when
- A confirmed double charge isn't reversed by the merchant within 10 business days.
