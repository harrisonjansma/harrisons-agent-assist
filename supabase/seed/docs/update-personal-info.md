# Updating Personal Information

Use this procedure when a customer needs to update their address, phone number, email, or name on the account.

## What can change
- Contact details (address, phone, email) update directly after identity verification.
- A legal name change requires supporting documentation.

## Steps
1. Verify the customer's identity before making any change.
2. In **Profile → Personal information**, update the field and confirm the new value back to the customer.
3. For an address change, ask whether to send a replacement card or new statements to the new address.

## Good to know
- Updating the email or phone may affect where security alerts and verification codes are sent — confirm the new contact works.
- A name change keeps the account and history intact; only the displayed name updates.

## Escalate when
- A legal name change requires document review the console can't process.
- The contact change looks like it may be an account-takeover attempt (unverified caller pushing to change email/phone).
