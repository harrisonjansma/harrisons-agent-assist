# Making a Payment

Use this procedure when a customer wants to make a one-time payment toward their card or account balance.

## Payment options
- Bank transfer (ACH) from a linked account, or a same-day payment where available.
- The customer can pay the minimum, the statement balance, the full current balance, or a custom amount.

## Steps
1. In **Payments → Make a payment**, select or add the funding bank account.
2. Choose the amount and the payment date (today or a future date before the due date).
3. Confirm and give the customer the confirmation number; note ACH payments typically post in 1-3 business days.

## Good to know
- Available credit may not update until the payment fully posts and clears.
- A future-dated payment can be edited or canceled until it begins processing.

## Escalate when
- A submitted payment doesn't post within 3 business days.
- The customer needs a same-day payment to avoid a late mark and the option isn't available to them.
