# Team Members and Permissions

Use this procedure when a creator wants to add a collaborator, an invite didn't arrive, or a team member can't access something they should.

## Roles
- **Owner:** full control including billing and deleting the workspace. One per workspace.
- **Editor:** can create, schedule, and publish posts and manage the Library.
- **Viewer:** read-only access to analytics and the Library.

## Steps to invite
1. In **Settings → Team**, have the owner click **Invite member** and enter the email and role.
2. The invitee gets an email link that's valid for 7 days; opening it joins them to the workspace.
3. Change or revoke a role anytime from the same page.

## Common issues
- **Invite didn't arrive:** check spam and confirm the address; resend from **Settings → Team**.
- **Editor can't reach billing:** billing is Owner-only by design — transfer ownership if needed.

## Escalate when
- The owner lost access and no other member can be promoted to Owner.
