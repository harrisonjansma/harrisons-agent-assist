# Payment Declined or Failed

Use this procedure when a customer's payment was declined or a scheduled payment returned unpaid.

## Common causes
- Non-sufficient funds in the linked bank account.
- Incorrect bank routing or account number, or a closed funding account.
- A bank-side block on the ACH draft.

## Steps
1. Open the payment and read the return reason code (NSF, invalid account, blocked).
2. For NSF, ask the customer to confirm funds and re-submit; for a bad account, re-verify the routing and account numbers.
3. Re-schedule the payment once the cause is fixed, and note whether a returned-payment fee applied.

## Preventing repeat failures
- Suggest linking a verified account and enabling low-balance awareness before autopay dates.
- Confirm the funding bank isn't blocking recurring ACH from us.

## Escalate when
- Payments keep failing from an account the customer insists is valid and funded.
- A returned payment caused a late mark the customer wants reviewed.
