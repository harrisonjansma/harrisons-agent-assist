# Best-Time Slot Suggestions

Use this procedure when a creator asks about the suggested posting times, or why a suggested slot changed.

## What it does
When scheduling a post, Shopfolio suggests **best-time slots** — windows when that creator's audience has historically been most active. It's a convenience on top of the manual queue, not an auto-poster; the creator always picks the final time.

## How suggestions are computed
- Slots are derived from the creator's own recent posting and click-through patterns. New accounts start from sensible defaults and personalize as data accumulates.
- Suggestions shift over time as engagement patterns change — a moved slot is expected, not a bug.

## Troubleshooting
1. **No suggestions yet:** the account is too new; defaults apply until there's enough history.
2. **Suggestions seem off:** confirm the workspace **timezone** is correct under **Settings → Workspace** — a wrong timezone shifts every slot.

## Notes
- Suggested slots feed the queue but don't change how publishing works — see *Scheduled Posts Not Publishing* for the hand-off flow.
