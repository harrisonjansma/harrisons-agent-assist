# Setting Up Autopay

Use this procedure when a customer wants to enroll in automatic payments, change the amount, or cancel autopay.

## Autopay options
- **Minimum due**, **statement balance**, or a **fixed amount** — the customer picks one.
- Autopay draws from a linked bank account on or just before the due date each cycle.

## Steps to enroll
1. In **Payments → Autopay**, verify the linked bank account (routing and account number) or add one.
2. Have the customer choose the pay amount and confirm the draft date.
3. Save and confirm the first autopay date; note that this cycle's payment may still need to be made manually if it's close to the due date.

## Changing or canceling
- Edits and cancellations take effect for the **next** cycle if made at least a few days before the draft date.
- Canceling autopay does not cancel a draft already scheduled within that window.

## Escalate when
- An autopay draft failed for non-sufficient funds and caused a returned-payment fee.
- The customer says autopay drew the wrong amount or from the wrong account.
