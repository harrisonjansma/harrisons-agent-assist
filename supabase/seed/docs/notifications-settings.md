# Notifications and Alerts

Use this procedure when a creator isn't getting Shopfolio notifications, or is getting too many.

## What we notify on
- Publish results (a scheduled post published or failed), broken-link detections, and team invites.
- Notifications go to email and, if the PWA is installed, as push alerts.

## Steps to configure
1. Open **Settings → Notifications** and review the per-event toggles for email and push.
2. To stop a category, turn off its toggle — changes save immediately.
3. For push, confirm the browser or phone granted notification permission to the installed app.

## Common issues
- **No emails at all:** confirm the account email is verified and check spam; our sends come from `notify@shopfolio.app`.
- **No push:** the PWA isn't installed or the OS blocked notifications — re-grant permission in system settings.

## Escalate when
- A creator has the toggles on and permissions granted but receives nothing across channels for over a day.
