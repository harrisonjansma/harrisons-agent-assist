# Credit Limit Increase Requests

Use this procedure when a customer asks to raise the credit limit on their card account.

## What to check first
- Confirm the account is in good standing — no recent late payments and not over the current limit.
- Ask whether they want a specific new limit and their current income, which the decision uses.

## Steps
1. In the servicing console, open **Credit line → Request increase** and enter the requested limit and updated income.
2. Tell the customer whether it's an instant decision or a review; some requests get an immediate result, others take up to 7-10 business days.
3. If approved, the new limit applies right away; if declined, an explanation letter is mailed.

## Good to know
- A request may involve a credit check; let the customer know if a hard inquiry applies.
- Frequent recent requests can lead to an automatic hold on new ones.

## Escalate when
- The customer disputes a decline reason or references a credit-report error.
- The requested increase is well above standard automatic-approval thresholds.
