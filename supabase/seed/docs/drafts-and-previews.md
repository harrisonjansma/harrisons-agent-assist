# Drafts and Post Previews

Use this procedure when a creator can't save a draft, their preview looks wrong, or a draft won't move to `scheduled`.

## How drafts work
- A post starts as a `draft` and stays private to the creator until it's scheduled or published.
- The preview panel renders the post exactly as the pin and storefront card will appear, including image crop and link.

## Steps
1. In the composer, have the creator fill title, image, and link, then click **Save draft**.
2. Use the **Preview** toggle to check the image crop and how the description wraps.
3. When ready, choose **Schedule** or **Publish now** to move the draft out of `draft`.

## Common issues
- **Preview image looks cropped:** Pinterest favors a 2:3 ratio — advise re-uploading a taller image.
- **Draft won't schedule:** a required field (usually the destination link) is empty; the composer flags it in red.

## Escalate when
- Saved drafts disappear from the Library after a page reload.
