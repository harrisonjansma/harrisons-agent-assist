# Activating a New Card

Use this procedure when a customer received a new or replacement card and needs to activate it.

## When activation is needed
- New accounts, renewals, and replacement cards all ship inactive and must be activated before use.
- Activating a replacement card also deactivates the old card number.

## Steps
1. Verify the customer's identity, then confirm the last four digits and expiration of the card in hand.
2. In **Card → Activate**, mark the card active; it's ready for use within a few minutes.
3. Remind the customer to sign the back and set or confirm their PIN if the card supports one.

## After activation
- Advise updating the new card number on any autopay or recurring merchants.
- If a chip or tap transaction fails at first, a swipe usually completes the first use and enables the chip.

## Escalate when
- The card won't activate because the account status blocks it (e.g. frozen or past due).
- The customer never received the card and needs a reissue.
