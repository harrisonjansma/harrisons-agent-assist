# Reporting a Lost or Stolen Card

Use this procedure when a customer reports their card lost, stolen, or misplaced and wants it secured.

## Immediate action
- Place a lock or block on the card right away to stop new authorizations.
- A lost/stolen report always issues a new card number; a temporary "misplaced" lock can be lifted if they find it.

## Steps
1. Verify the customer's identity, then set the card status to **lost/stolen** in the servicing console.
2. Ask whether they see any charges they don't recognize — if so, route those to the fraud team.
3. Order a replacement card and confirm the mailing address; standard delivery is 5-7 business days, expedited 1-2.

## After reporting
- Remind the customer to update the new card on any autopay or recurring merchants once it arrives.
- Their account and history carry over; only the card number changes.

## Escalate when
- The customer reports unauthorized charges alongside the lost card (fraud handling).
- They need emergency card access while traveling with no mailing address.
