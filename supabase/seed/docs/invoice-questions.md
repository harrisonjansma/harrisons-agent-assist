# Invoice and Billing Questions

Use this when a customer has questions about an invoice, receipt, tax, or needs billing details changed.

## Finding invoices
- All invoices and receipts live under **Account → Billing → Invoices**. Each can be downloaded as PDF.
- If the customer can't log in, verify identity and resend a specific invoice from **Admin → Billing → Resend invoice**.

## Common questions
- **"What is this charge?"** Match the date and amount to a transaction. Renewals bill on the anniversary of signup; proration lines appear after a mid-cycle plan change (see Plan Upgrades and Downgrades).
- **Tax/VAT:** tax is calculated from the billing address and local rules. If a business customer needs their VAT/Tax ID on invoices, add it under **Account → Billing → Tax details**; invoices going forward will include it.
- **Company name / address wrong:** update billing details under **Account → Billing**; offer to reissue the most recent invoice with corrected details.
- **Currency:** the account's billing currency is set at signup and generally can't be changed without creating a new subscription.

## Reissuing and adjustments
- You can reissue an invoice with corrected billing details, but you cannot change the amount of a settled invoice — that requires a refund or credit.
- Apply account credits from **Billing → Adjustments** with a clear note.

## What to tell the customer
- Point them to where the document lives so they can self-serve next time.
- Confirm any change will appear on future invoices.

## Escalate when
- The customer needs a formal tax document we don't generate automatically.
- There's a billing discrepancy you can't reconcile against the transaction record.
