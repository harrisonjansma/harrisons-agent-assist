# Rewards and Cashback Redemption

Use this procedure when a customer wants to redeem rewards or cashback, or their balance looks wrong.

## How rewards work
- Rewards accrue on eligible purchases and post after the transaction fully clears.
- Pending rewards on unposted or returned transactions may adjust before they finalize.

## Steps to redeem
1. In **Rewards**, confirm the redeemable balance (excluding pending).
2. Choose a redemption method — statement credit, bank deposit, or gift options where available.
3. Confirm; statement credits typically apply within 1-2 billing cycles, deposits in a few business days.

## Balance looks off
- Purchases that were refunded claw back the rewards they earned.
- Some categories or transaction types (cash advances, fees) don't earn rewards.

## Escalate when
- Posted rewards are missing for eligible purchases across a full cycle.
- The customer disputes a rewards clawback they believe is incorrect.
