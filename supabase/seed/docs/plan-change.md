# Plan Upgrades and Downgrades

Use this when a customer wants to move between plans (for example Basic, Pro, and Team).

## Upgrades
- Upgrades take effect immediately. The customer gets the new features right away.
- Billing is **prorated**: they're charged the difference for the remainder of the current period, then the full new rate at the next renewal.
- Change the plan under **Account → Subscription → Change plan**. Confirm the prorated amount shown before applying.

## Downgrades
- Downgrades take effect at the **end of the current billing period**, so the customer keeps paid-for features until then.
- Warn about anything they'll lose on the lower plan — seats, storage, advanced features, or higher limits — before confirming.
- If they're downgrading to reduce cost, this is often a good alternative to full cancellation (see Cancelling a Subscription).

## Annual vs monthly
- Switching monthly → annual applies immediately and usually lowers the effective rate; proration credits the unused monthly time.
- Switching annual → monthly applies at renewal, not mid-term.

## Team/seat changes
- Adding seats is prorated and immediate. Removing seats applies at period end.
- Only the account owner or an admin can change the Team plan.

## Confirm
- State the new price, when it takes effect, and the next billing date.
- Note that a confirmation email will follow.

## Escalate when
- The customer is on a custom/enterprise contract — those pricing changes go through their account manager.
- A promised discount or promo isn't applying (see Promo Code Problems).
