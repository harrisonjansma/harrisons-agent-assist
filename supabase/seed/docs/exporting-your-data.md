# Exporting Your Data

Use this procedure when a creator wants to export their catalog, links, or analytics — or asks whether they can leave without losing anything.

## What's exportable
Portability is a core promise: **everything is exportable.** A creator can download their products, storefronts, link map (including `/r/<code>` codes), and click analytics at any time.

## How to export
1. In **Settings → Data → Export**, choose what to include (library, storefronts, links, analytics).
2. Generate the export. For large accounts it's prepared asynchronously and emailed as a download link when ready.
3. The bundle is CSV + JSON so it can be re-imported elsewhere or kept as a backup.

## Retargeting instead of leaving
If the creator only wants to move where links point (not leave), note that **destinations are retargetable** — they can bulk-swap a link's destination without changing the public `/r/<code>` URL, so their bio links keep working.

## What to tell the creator
- Reassure them there's no lock-in and no export fee.
- If they're exporting because something's broken, capture the underlying issue too.
