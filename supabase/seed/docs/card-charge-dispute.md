# Disputing a Card Charge

Use this procedure when a customer wants to dispute a charge they believe is wrong — an incorrect amount, a service not received, or a charge they don't recognize.

## Before opening a dispute
- Have the customer confirm the merchant, date, and amount from their statement.
- Ask whether they contacted the merchant first — many billing errors resolve faster that way.

## Steps
1. Open the transaction in the servicing console and select **Start dispute**.
2. Choose a reason code (billing error, goods not received, service not as described) and capture the customer's description.
3. Issue a provisional credit if eligible, and let the customer know the case number and that a decision typically takes up to 90 days.

## While the dispute is open
- The disputed amount may be temporarily credited; interest on it is paused pending the outcome.
- The customer can add documentation (receipts, emails) to the case at any time.

## Escalate when
- The customer reports the transaction as fraud rather than a billing error (route to the fraud team).
- The disputed amount is large or the merchant has already refused a refund in writing.
