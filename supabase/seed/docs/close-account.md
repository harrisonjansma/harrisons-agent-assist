# Closing an Account

Use this procedure when a customer wants to close their card or bank account.

## Before closing
- Confirm the balance is zero — a card with an outstanding balance can't be closed until it's paid.
- Ask about pending transactions, autopay, and recurring charges tied to the account.

## Steps
1. Verify identity and confirm the customer is the account holder.
2. Check for a remaining balance, pending charges, or unredeemed rewards, and resolve each first.
3. In **Account → Close account**, submit the closure and give the customer written confirmation and the effective date.

## After closing
- Advise moving any recurring merchants to another payment method to avoid failed charges.
- A closed account may still show on the credit report as closed in good standing.

## Escalate when
- The customer wants to close while disputing a balance or fee.
- There are pending transactions or a credit balance owed back to the customer.
