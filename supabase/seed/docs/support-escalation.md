# Support Escalation and Outages

Use this procedure to decide when to escalate a Shopfolio support case beyond first-line help.

## Escalate immediately
- **Storefronts down for many creators at once** — a custom-domain edge or certificate incident affecting more than one account.
- **The publish queue is backed up across creators** — jobs stuck `processing` or `pending` platform-wide (see *Scheduled Posts Not Publishing*).
- **A real billing charge naming Shopfolio** — even though billing isn't live (see *Billing and Subscription*).
- **Any suspected data exposure or account takeover.**

## Handle first, escalate only if unresolved
- Single custom-domain SSL/DNS setup (see *Storefront SSL / TLS Certificate Issues*) — escalate only if a corrected, unproxied record still won't provision after 2+ hours.
- Individual capture, import, or analytics quirks.

## What to include when you escalate
1. Creator's account email and the affected domain or post/link.
2. Exact state shown in the dashboard (`DNS pending`, `verifying`, `pending`, `failed`, etc.) and timestamps.
3. What you already tried and the result.

## Tone
Lead with the impact on the creator's audience or drop, not the internal cause. Give a concrete next-check time rather than an open-ended "we're looking into it."
