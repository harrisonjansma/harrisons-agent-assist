# Balance Transfers

Use this procedure when a customer wants to move a balance from another card onto this account, or is asking about one in progress.

## How it works
- A balance transfer pays off another creditor and moves that balance here, often at a promotional APR for a set period.
- A transfer fee (a percentage of the amount) usually applies and posts with the transfer.

## Steps
1. Confirm available credit is enough to cover the transfer plus the fee.
2. In **Payments → Balance transfer**, enter the other creditor's details and the amount, and disclose the fee and promo terms.
3. Submit and set expectations: transfers commonly take 5-14 business days to reach the other creditor.

## Good to know
- Advise the customer to keep paying the other card until the transfer confirms, to avoid a late fee there.
- The promotional APR ends on a set date, after which the standard APR applies to any remaining balance.

## Escalate when
- The transfer hasn't reached the other creditor after 14 business days.
- The customer disputes the fee or the promotional terms applied.
