# Billing and Subscription

Use this procedure when a creator asks about their plan, price, or a billing charge.

## How pricing works
Shopfolio is a **flat subscription** — one price for the product, independent of how many links, clicks, or sales a creator has. Monetization features (associate tags) are a demoted convenience, never a revenue share; Shopfolio takes no cut of a creator's earnings.

## Current state
Billing is currently **not live** — the product is in an access phase and paid subscriptions aren't being charged yet. If a creator sees an unexpected charge attributed to Shopfolio, it is not from us; help them identify the real merchant and, if needed, escalate.

## Common questions
1. **"What will it cost?"** A single flat subscription; the plan page shows the current number.
2. **"Do you take a percentage of my sales?"** No. Pricing is flat and revenue is not tracked at all (see *Link Click Analytics*).
3. **"How do I cancel / export first?"** Nothing is being billed today; for data portability see *Exporting Your Data*.

## Escalate when
- A creator reports a real charge that names Shopfolio.
