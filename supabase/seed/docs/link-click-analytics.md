# Link Click Analytics

Use this procedure when a creator asks about clicks, why a link shows no data, or what the analytics do and don't track.

## What we track
Every storefront and post link is served through a tracked `/r/<code>` redirect. We count **click-throughs** — opens per link, over time, by referrer. That's it.

## What we do NOT track
Shopfolio does **not** track sales, revenue, or dollar amounts. Click analytics are revenue-free by design. If a creator expects to see earnings, explain that monetization is intentionally out of scope and only click data is available.

## Troubleshooting "no clicks"
1. Confirm the link in the creator's bio/post is the `/r/<code>` short link, not the raw destination URL — raw links bypass tracking.
2. Analytics roll up on a short delay; brand-new links can read zero for a few minutes.
3. Ad blockers and some in-app browsers suppress the redirect ping, so counts are a floor, not an exact number.

## Escalate when
- A link that clearly gets traffic reports zero for more than an hour.
