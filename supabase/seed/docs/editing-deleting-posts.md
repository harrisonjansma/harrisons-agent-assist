# Editing and Deleting Posts

Use this procedure when a creator wants to change or remove a post, or a deleted post still appears on their storefront or Pinterest.

## What editing changes
- Edits to a `draft` or `scheduled` post apply immediately and take effect at publish time.
- Edits to an already-`published` post update the storefront copy, but do **not** rewrite the pin already live on Pinterest.

## Steps
1. Open **Library**, find the post, and choose **Edit** to change title, image, link, or schedule.
2. To remove a post, choose **Delete** — this removes it from Shopfolio and the storefront.
3. Deleting does not retract a pin already published to Pinterest; the creator must delete that pin in Pinterest directly.

## Common issues
- **Deleted post still shows on the storefront:** the storefront cache can lag a few minutes; a hard refresh clears it.
- **Edit button greyed out:** the post is mid-publish (`sending`) — wait for the job to settle, then edit.

## Escalate when
- A deleted post reappears after the cache window, suggesting a sync issue.
