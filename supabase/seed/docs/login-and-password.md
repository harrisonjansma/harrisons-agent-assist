# Login and Password Help

Use this procedure when a creator can't sign in, didn't get a reset or magic-link email, or is locked out.

## Sign-in basics
Shopfolio uses Supabase Auth. Creators sign in with email + password or a magic link. Sessions persist across devices until they sign out or the session expires.

## Reset a password
1. On the sign-in page, have them choose **Forgot password** and enter the email on the account.
2. They'll get a reset link. If it doesn't arrive in a few minutes, check spam and confirm they used the exact email on the account (not an alias).
3. The link is single-use and time-limited; if it expired, request a fresh one.

## Magic link didn't arrive
- Same checks: spam folder, correct address, and that the link hasn't already been opened once.
- Corporate mail filters sometimes hold the message — try a personal address if one is on the account.

## Locked out / no email access
- Verify identity against the account details, then trigger a reset to a confirmed address.

## Escalate when
- The creator no longer controls the email on the account (ownership change).
