# Statement and Transaction Questions

Use this procedure when a customer has questions about a statement — an unfamiliar merchant name, a balance that doesn't add up, or where to find a past statement.

## Reading the statement
- Statements cover one billing cycle and show the previous balance, payments, new charges, interest, fees, and the new balance.
- Merchant names on a statement often differ from the storefront name (a parent company or payment processor).

## Steps
1. Open the statement in the servicing console and walk the customer through the line item in question.
2. For an unfamiliar merchant, look up the descriptor — many resolve to a recognizable parent company.
3. To retrieve an old statement, pull it from **Documents → Statements**; up to 24 months are available.

## If the charge is genuinely unknown
- If the customer still doesn't recognize it after checking the descriptor, treat it as a possible dispute or fraud.

## Escalate when
- The math on the statement appears wrong (interest or fees not matching the terms).
- The customer identifies a charge as unauthorized (route per fraud handling).
