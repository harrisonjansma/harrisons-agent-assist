# Fixing Broken or 404 Links

Use this procedure when a creator's product link returns a 404, redirects to a homepage, or shows as `broken` in their Library.

## How link checking works
- Shopfolio periodically re-checks destination links and flags each as `ok`, `redirected`, or `broken` on the Library card.
- A `broken` flag means the merchant returned a 404 or the URL no longer resolves.

## Steps to fix
1. Open **Library** and filter by **Link status → Broken**.
2. For each item, open the destination to confirm it's dead, then paste a working replacement URL and save.
3. If the product moved, capture the new page with the Chrome extension to refresh image and price too.

## Common issues
- **`redirected` but works:** the merchant added tracking or region redirects — usually safe to leave.
- **Whole retailer flagged:** the merchant changed its URL structure; use bulk retargeting (see *Bulk Editing and Retargeting Links*).

## Escalate when
- Links report `broken` in Shopfolio but load fine in a browser (possible checker false-positive).
